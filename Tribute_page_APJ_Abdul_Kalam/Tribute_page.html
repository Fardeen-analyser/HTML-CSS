Tribute to APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India,"was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameshwaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space and missile programs.

Achievements:

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020".
  • Recieved the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.
